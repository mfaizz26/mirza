About Sony

Founded in 1946 in Tokyo by Masaru Ibuka and Akio Morita, Sony started as a small electronics shop and evolved into a global powerhouse. Its early innovation, such as Japan’s first transistor radio and the Walkman, shaped consumer technology as we know it.

Today, Sony's reach spans industries—from electronics to entertainment, from gaming to financial services. With its mission to "fill the world with emotion through the power of creativity and technology," Sony is both a cultural force and a technology pioneer.

Our Services

  • Electronics: Sony is known for premium TVs, cameras, headphones, and smartphones that combine aesthetics and function.
  • Gaming: Through the PlayStation platform, Sony has revolutionized interactive entertainment globally.
  • Music & Movies: Sony Music and Sony Pictures have launched iconic artists and blockbuster films.
  • Anime: With Crunchyroll, Sony leads the global anime streaming scene.
  • Financial Services: Sony offers banking, insurance, and fintech solutions primarily in Japan.

Portfolio Highlights

  • Sony WH Series: Market-leading noise-canceling headphones praised for comfort and sound.
  • Alpha Cameras: Mirrorless camera innovation for photographers and videographers.
  • Spider-Man Franchise: Cinematic success under Sony Pictures with Marvel collaboration.
  • Vision-S Concept EV: Sony’s leap into smart mobility, blending entertainment and transport tech.
